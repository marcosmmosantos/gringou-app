Build a single-page promotional website in Brazilian Portuguese for an app called **Gringou**. Follow every detail below exactly.

---

**WHAT IS GRINGOU?**
Gringou is a companion app for Brazilian students moving to Ireland. It covers the full journey: pre-arrival planning (visa, documents) through settling in (PPS number, IRP card, housing, transport, culture, community). The name comes from "ir pra gringa" — "gringou" means you made the leap. The app is not live yet. The site's main goal is to collect waitlist emails while showcasing what the app will do.

---

**BRAND PERSONALITY**
Warm, calm, organised — like a Brazilian friend who moved to Ireland 2 years before you. Not a corporation, not a government portal. A peer who's been through it all. All copy is in Brazilian Portuguese only.
